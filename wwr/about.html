About Whitewater Rafting

Whitewater rafting is an exhilarating outdoor activity that involves navigating through turbulent river waters in an inflatable raft. It combines adventure, teamwork, and a deep connection with nature, making it a popular choice for thrill-seekers and nature enthusiasts alike.

History

The history of whitewater rafting dates back to the early 19th century, when explorers and adventurers began navigating untamed rivers for transportation and discovery. Early rafts were handmade and used out of necessity.

Over the years, rafting evolved into a recreational activity. By the 1960s, commercial rafting companies began offering guided trips, making it accessible to the public. Today it's a thrilling experience enjoyed worldwide!
